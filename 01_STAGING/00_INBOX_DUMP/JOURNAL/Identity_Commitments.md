# Identity Commitments

This document defines who I am becoming through repeated action.

## Identity Before Outcomes
- I act first; motivation follows
- I keep promises to myself
- I show up regardless of mood

## Personal Laws
- Action > Emotion
- Discipline is non-negotiable
- Consistency is returning after failure

## Identity Statements
- I am someone who does hard things daily
- I respect my word to myself
- I build before I speak
- I improve quietly

## Self-Trust
- Willpower erodes when promises are broken
- Confidence is earned through follow-through
- Every completed task is a vote for my future self

## Emotional Integrity
- Discomfort is information, not a threat
- Avoidance is a signal to move forward
- Growth requires solitude and silence
