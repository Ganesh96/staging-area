# Action Plan - Establish Daily Routine

## Goal

Create a consistent routine to improve productivity and well-being.

---

## Steps

- Define wake-up time (e.g. 5:00 AM).
- Implement 5 AM Club activities:
  - Move (exercise)
  - Reflect (journal or meditate)
  - Grow (learning)
- Block deep work sessions mid-morning.
- Implement digital sunset after 9 PM.
- Set evening shutdown routine.

---

## Metrics

- Consistency of wake-up time.
- % of days routine followed.
