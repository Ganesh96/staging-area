## Interview Preparation Tips

- Review past projects involving Windows, web, and database technologies.
- Practice coding exercises in C# and VB.NET.
- Revise common design patterns used in .NET.
- Strengthen understanding of RESTful APIs.
- Be prepared to discuss healthcare-related project challenges and solutions.
