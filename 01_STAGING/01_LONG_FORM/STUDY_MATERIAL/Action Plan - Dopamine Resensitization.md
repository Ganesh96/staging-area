# Action Plan - Dopamine Resensitization

## Goal

Reduce overstimulation and rebuild sensitivity to natural rewards.

---

## Steps

- Identify high-dopamine triggers (social media, junk food).
- Schedule boredom breaks (walks, meditation).
- Replace scrolling with low-dopamine alternatives (reading, journaling).
- Avoid screen use during first hour after waking and one hour before bed.
- Log urges and replacement actions.

---

## Metrics

- Number of urges successfully redirected.
- Average daily screen time.
