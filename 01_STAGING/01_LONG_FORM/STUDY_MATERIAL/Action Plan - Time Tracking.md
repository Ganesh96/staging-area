# Action Plan - Time Tracking

## Goal

Identify time leaks and improve focus by tracking how each hour is spent.

---

## Steps

- Choose a tracking method (journal, app, spreadsheet).
- Log tasks every hour for 7 days.
- Analyze:
  - Energy peaks
  - Focus dips
  - Frequent distractions
- Design new schedule based on patterns.

---

## Metrics

- % of day tracked successfully.
- Identified hours of peak flow potential.
