# Book Summary - Atomic Habits

## Core Ideas

- Tiny changes compound into significant results.
- Focus on systems rather than goals alone.
- Habit loop: cue → craving → response → reward.

---

## Favorite Quotes

> “You do not rise to the level of your goals. You fall to the level of your systems.”

> “Habits are the compound interest of self-improvement.”

---

## Applications

- Use habit stacking.
- Implement Seinfeld Strategy.
- Track habits visually.
