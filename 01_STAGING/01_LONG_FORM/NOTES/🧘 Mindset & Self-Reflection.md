# 🧘 Mindset & Self-Reflection

## Mindset Foundations
- “I am not my mistakes. I am the lessons I learn.”
- “My worth is my birthright, not a reward.”
- "Discomfort is growth’s price."

## Daily Reflection
- End each day: accomplishments, lessons, peak productivity.
- Use journaling to build radical self-compassion.
- Tools: 5 Whys, Cynefin Framework, OODA Loop, THINK Method.

## Emotional Management
- Acknowledge stress triggers
- Replace coping (scrolling, porn) with mindful response
- Practice emotional honesty and forgiveness

## Holistic Practices
- Wake early, eat healthy, exercise
- Spend time in nature
- Own only useful items (minimalism)
- Speak clearly, manage anger, negotiate well
- Play strategy games (football, Shogi)

> Weekly: Review journal, assess progress, set next week’s goals.
