# 🎯 Goal Setting & Self-Improvement

## Goal Setting
- **S.M.A.R.T. Goals**: Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound.
- Write 1-2 SMART goals each evening.
- Example: “30-minute cardio at 7 AM” > “Exercise”

## Mindset Shifts
- "Life doesn’t owe you anything. You earn it."
- “Winning is a by-product of honest work.”
- Control what you can; ignore what you can't.
- Positivity: Push for 5 more minutes when tired.

## Desired Traits
- Self-respect, deep thinker, goal-oriented
- Financial + technical acumen
- Embraces long-term planning
- Authentic: Focus on personal interests and not others’ judgment
