# Focus

Focus is the ability to channel attention toward value and away from distraction.

## Why Focus Matters
- Focus multiplies output
- Context switching drains energy
- Multitasking is cognitive self-sabotage

## Principles
- Do one thing at a time
- Externalize distractions
- Reduce stimulation outside work to increase focus inside work

## Focus as a Skill
- Focus improves through deliberate training
- Short, intense sessions beat long distracted ones
- Boredom tolerance increases attention span

## Environmental Control
- Remove friction for important tasks
- Increase friction for distractions
- Design surroundings to make the right action easier

## Focus Hierarchy
1. Vision (why)
2. Priority (what matters most)
3. Action (what now)
