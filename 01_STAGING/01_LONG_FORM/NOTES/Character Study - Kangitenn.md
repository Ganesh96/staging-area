# Character Study - Kangiten

Kangiten represents a philosophy of transforming adversity into strength. Inspired by the notion that faults and weaknesses can become one’s weapons.

---

## Core Principles

- “Failure and frustration are sources of growth.”
- “Your faults and weaknesses can be your weapons.”
- True strength comes not from being flawless but from deeply understanding your flaws and using them as advantages.
- Related to the Stoic concept that “The obstacle is the way.”
