# Book Summary - Grit

## Core Ideas

- Passion and perseverance matter more than talent.
- Grit grows through purpose and practice.

---

## Favorite Quotes

> “Enthusiasm is common. Endurance is rare.”

> “Grit is living life like it’s a marathon, not a sprint.”

---

## Applications

- Reflect on long-term goals regularly.
- View effort as essential to achievement.
- Build resilience through deliberate practice.
