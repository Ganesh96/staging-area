# Purpose and Meaning

A meaningful life is not found through comfort or happiness chasing, but through responsibility, service, and becoming capable of carrying weight.

## Core Principles
- Meaning comes from **serving something larger than yourself**
- Happiness is a side effect, not a goal
- Growth requires voluntary confrontation with difficulty
- If you are not working toward your ideal future, you are working toward someone else’s

## Meaning Over Happiness
- Chasing happiness leads to fragility
- Meaning sustains effort through suffering
- Hard work feels lighter when it serves purpose

## Ownership and Responsibility
- The world does not owe fairness
- Responsibility creates agency
- Self-respect is built by keeping promises to yourself

## Directional Living
- Nothing is neutral
- Every action moves you toward or away from your values
- Ask: *“What would the ideal version of me do?”*

## Long-Term Framing
- Sacrifice short-term comfort for long-term clarity
- Choose progress over validation
- Measure success by impact, not applause
