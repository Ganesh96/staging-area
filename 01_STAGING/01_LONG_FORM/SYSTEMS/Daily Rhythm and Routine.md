# Daily Rhythm and Routine

A structured daily plan to maximize focus, energy, and fulfillment.

---

## Morning Routine (5 AM Club)

- **5:00 – 5:20 AM** → Move (exercise)
- **5:20 – 5:40 AM** → Reflect (journaling, meditation)
- **5:40 – 6:00 AM** → Grow (reading, learning)

---

## Work Blocks

- **Deep Work Blocks**
   - Pomodoro sprints
   - Avoid context switching
- **Shallow Work Blocks**
   - Email, admin, meetings
   - Batched together

---

## Evening Routine

- Shutdown ritual
- Plan tomorrow, tonight
- Digital sunset (disconnect 1 hr before bed)
