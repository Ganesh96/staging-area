# Lessons, Mantras, and How to Apply in Life

Mantras and mindset shifts for daily living.

---

## Core Mantras

- “Life doesn’t owe you anything.”
- “Winning is a by-product of honest work.”
- “Control what you can; ignore what you can’t.”
- “Discomfort is growth’s price.”

---

## How to Apply

- Daily journaling.
- Focus on one meaningful task each day.
- Live forward, understand backward.
- Embrace small progress.
