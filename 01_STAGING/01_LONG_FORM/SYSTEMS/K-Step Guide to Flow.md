# K-Step Guide to Flow

A systematic approach to consistently achieving flow and peak performance.

---

## Core Steps

1. **Know Thyself**
   - Identify chronotype and energy peaks.
   - Audit digital distractions and friction.

2. **Design Your Day**
   - Use time blocking.
   - Separate planning vs. doing.
   - Create a “Flow Dojo” workspace.

3. **Enter Flow**
   - Embrace the struggle phase.
   - Take strategic breaks.
   - Return to deep focus.

4. **Leverage Flow Cycle**
   - Struggle → Release → Flow → Recovery.

5. **Sleep as a Skill**
   - Practice the 80/20 sleep protocol (darkness, temperature, routine).

6. **Active Recovery**
   - Cold plunges, sauna, nature walks, stretching.

7. **Avoid Procrastination**
   - “First action so easy” technique.
   - 60-second rule.
   - Microscopic goals.
