# How to Avoid Craving and Procrastination

Tactics to overcome dopamine-driven distraction and procrastination.

---

## Dopamine Insights

- Overstimulation leads to desensitization.
- Long-term habit change > short-term detox.

---

## Resensitization Strategies

- Take “boring” breaks.
- Avoid high-dopamine distractions.
- Focus on single tasks.

---

## Procrastination Tactics

- 60-Second Rule: Start for 2 minutes.
- Label fears out loud.
- 10-10-10 Rule: Reframe setbacks.
- Batman Effect: Use alter egos.

---

## Purpose and Goals

- Connect daily tasks to your big “why.”
- Schedule meaningful tasks during peak energy.
