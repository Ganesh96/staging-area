# Inbox Protocol

Put raw unprocessed material in `00_INBOX_DUMP/`.

Use filenames:
YYYY-MM-DD_source_topic.md

Examples:
2026-05-01_keep_random-notes.md
2026-05-01_obsidian_decision-theory.md
2026-05-01_chatgpt_prompt-system.md

Do not organize while dumping.
Do not clean while capturing.
Do not delete during intake.
