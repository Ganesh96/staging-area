# Character Study - Kangiten

Kangiten represents a powerful philosophy of turning adversity into strength, a key concept in [[2. Mindset and Mental Models]].

### Core Principles of Kangiten
- **"Failure and Frustration are sources of growth."** Do not view setbacks as absolute negatives. They are data points for learning and opportunities to build resilience.
- **"Your faults and weaknesses can be your weapons."** True strength comes not from being flawless, but from understanding your weaknesses so well that you can turn them into advantages or render them irrelevant.

This philosophy is directly related to the Stoic idea of *The Obstacle Is the Way* mentioned in [[6. Recommended Reading]].
