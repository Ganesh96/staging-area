# Book Summary - Essentialism

## Core Ideas

- Do less, but better.
- Focus only on what’s truly essential.
- Eliminate non-essentials ruthlessly.

---

## Favorite Quotes

> “If you don’t prioritize your life, someone else will.”

---

## Applications

- Use the 90% rule: if it’s not a 9/10 in importance, eliminate it.
- Regularly review commitments.
- Create space for deep work.
