# Book Summary - Deep Work

## Core Ideas

- Deep Work = distraction-free concentration that pushes cognitive capabilities.
- Shallow work = non-cognitively demanding tasks that produce low value.
- Success in knowledge work increasingly depends on the ability to perform deep work.

---

## Favorite Quotes

> “Clarity about what matters provides clarity about what does not.”

> “A deep life is a good life.”

---

## Applications

- Block 2-hour deep work sessions each morning.
- Schedule shallow tasks separately.
- Digital detox periods to train focus.
