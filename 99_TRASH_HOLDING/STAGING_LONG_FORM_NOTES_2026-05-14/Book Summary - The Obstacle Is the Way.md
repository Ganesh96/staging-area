# Book Summary - The Obstacle Is the Way

## Core Ideas

- Every obstacle contains a hidden path forward.
- Perception → Action → Will:
  - Change how you view adversity.
  - Take effective action.
  - Build resilience through inner will.

---

## Favorite Quotes

> “The obstacle in the path becomes the path.”

> “What stands in the way becomes the way.”

---

## Applications

- Reframe setbacks as training.
- Seek opportunity in challenges.
- Practice mental stillness under pressure.
