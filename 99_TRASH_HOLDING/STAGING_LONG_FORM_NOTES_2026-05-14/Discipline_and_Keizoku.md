# Discipline and Keizoku

Discipline is choosing what you want most over what you want now.

## Keizoku (継続)
- Continuation despite discomfort
- Progress through persistence, not intensity

## Discipline First
- Action precedes motivation
- Mood is irrelevant to execution

## Micro Discipline
- Do one avoided task daily
- Prepare tomorrow the night before
- Lower activation energy relentlessly

## Physical Discipline
- Body training strengthens mental resolve
- Movement stabilizes mood and focus

## Rule
Consistency does not guarantee success.
Inconsistency guarantees failure.
