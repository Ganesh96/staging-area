# Feynman Technique (Study Method)

**Principle:**  
Explain a concept as if the listener knows *nothing* about it.

---

## Steps

1. Take a blank piece of paper.
2. Choose a single concept.
3. Write down a clear definition of the concept.
4. Add a simple, concrete example.
5. Break the concept down into its simplest components.
6. Explain everything using plain, simple words.

---

## Goal

- Identify gaps in understanding  
- Replace complexity with clarity  
- Ensure true comprehension, not memorization

---

## Note
If you struggle to explain any part simply, that part needs further study.
