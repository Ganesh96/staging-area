# Time Architecture

Time should be designed, not reacted to.

## Core Idea
Energy, not hours, is the true unit of productivity.

## Principles
- Plan before acting
- Protect high-energy periods
- Batch shallow work

## Time Structuring Tools
- Time blocking
- Focus sprints
- Two-calendar method (planned vs actual)

## Strategic Time Use
- Schedule leverage tasks first
- Accept that good prioritization feels uncomfortable
- Doing less, better, over longer periods wins

## Temporal Awareness
- Track time to reveal reality
- Redesign schedule based on energy patterns
