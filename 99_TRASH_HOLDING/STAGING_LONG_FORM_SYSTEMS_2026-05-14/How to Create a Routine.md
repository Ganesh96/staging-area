# How to Create a Routine

Routines are the backbone of discipline and progress.

---

## Core Steps

1. Define your desired identity.
2. Establish anchors:
   - Wake-up time
   - Morning rituals
   - Bedtime
3. Create minimum viable routines:
   - Even a 5-minute start is progress.
4. Use triggers:
   - “After X, I will Y.”
5. Track and iterate.
