# 🧠 Productivity & Time Management

## Key Frameworks
- **Procrastination Framework**: Break large tasks into small steps. Use rewards/penalties for accountability.
- **Time Blocking**: Schedule blocks for deep work, meetings, etc. (e.g., 9–11 AM for deep work).
- **Pomodoro Technique**: 25-minute work sprints + 5-minute breaks. Long break after 4 Pomodoros.
- **Eisenhower Matrix**:
  - Urgent & Important → Do now
  - Important, Not Urgent → Schedule
  - Urgent, Not Important → Delegate
  - Not Urgent & Not Important → Eliminate
- **2-Minute Rule**: If a task takes <2 mins, do it immediately.
- **Task Batching**: Group similar tasks to minimize context switching.
- **Kanban Board**: Visual task management — “To Do”, “In Progress”, “Done”.

## Applying Strategies to Your Day
- **Morning**: Clear quick tasks (2-Minute Rule), start deep work during peak time.
- **Mid-Morning**: Focused Pomodoro sessions for high-priority work.
- **Afternoon**: Lighter tasks, Eisenhower-based prioritization.
- **End of Day**: Review tasks, journal accomplishments, plan next day.

> Tools: Habitica, Google Calendar, whiteboard/Kanban tools
