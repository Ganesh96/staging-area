# ⚙️ Habit Formation & Personal Discipline

## Core Habit Strategies
- **Cue-Routine-Reward** loop to form habits.
- **Seinfeld Strategy**: Mark every day you complete a habit. Never break the chain.
- **Start Small**: Don’t overcommit. Build gradually.
- **Snowballing**: Stack new habits onto existing ones.
- **Reminders**: Use phone alarms or sticky notes.
- **Replace Negative Triggers**: Replace bad habits (e.g., scrolling) with positive triggers (e.g., 5 push-ups).

## Personal Discipline Archetype: *Kangiten Uchiha*
- Wakes early, trains, meditates, manages time/money.
- Keeps promises, maintains hygiene.
- Controlled ego and anger.
- Goal-driven and self-respecting.

> Principle: "Discipline is choosing what you want most over what you want now."
