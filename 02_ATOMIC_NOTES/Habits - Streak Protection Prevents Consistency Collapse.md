# Habits - Streak Protection Prevents Consistency Collapse

## Core idea
Consistency is better protected by streak rules and habit stacking than by intensity bursts.

## Explanation
“Don’t break the chain,” “never miss two days,” and stack-on-existing-habit patterns reduce reset risk.

## Use case
Use for long-term skill building where daily continuity matters.
