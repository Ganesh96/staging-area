# Interview Prep - Company Prep Must Separate Process Patterns and Execution

## Core idea
Interview prep improves when you separate three layers: process (how prep is run), patterns (what skills are trained), and execution (what is done today).

## Use case
Design weekly plans that avoid resource sprawl and checklist-only progress.
