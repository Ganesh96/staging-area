# Interviewing - STAR Response Structure Increases Signal

## Core idea
Behavioral answers are stronger when structured with STAR and centered on concrete actions and outcomes.

## Explanation
Raw prompts repeatedly emphasize examples, reflection, and impact. STAR enforces that shape and prevents vague storytelling.

## Use case
Prepare reusable interview stories for conflict, deadline misses, leadership, and adaptability.
